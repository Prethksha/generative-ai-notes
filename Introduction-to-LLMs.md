# Large Language Models (LLMs)
## What are LLMs?
- Large Language Models are AI models trained on large amounts of text data.

## Examples
- GPT
- Gemini
- Claude
- Llama

## Capabilities
- Answer Questions
- Summarization
- Translation
- Coding Assistance
- Content Generation

## Limitations
- Hallucinations
- Bias
- Outdated Knowledge

  
##Key concepts
1. Large language models understand and generate natural language, enable businesses to automate complex language tasks, and enhance customer experiences across various applications.
2. IBM Granite models deliver tailored AI solutions that address specific business needs. They provide reliable and efficient results for diverse industries through capabilities such as language processing, multilingual support, code generation, and content safety.
3. Effective prompting techniques, such as clarity, specificity, and providing examples, help guide LLMs to deliver accurate results for targeted tasks.
4. Using IBM Granite models in IBM watsonx Prompt Lab, developers can craft and refine prompts to achieve effective output from LLMs.
